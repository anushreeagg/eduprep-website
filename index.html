Our Mission

Through mindful AI-driven test prep that never reveals answers directly, EduPrep AI guides every student toward independent thinking and deep mastery, allowing them to learn effectively at their own pace and style.

We're eliminating the need for afterschool private coaching by ensuring schools finally deliver personalized feedback, deep understanding, and the space to learn at every student's own pace.

Our Vision

We're building a future where AI enhances and never replaces learning. A future where no child feels inadequate because the classroom moved too fast, and after-school coaching becomes obsolete.

Our AI asks better questions instead of giving answers, helping students think critically, while teachers are freed from mechanical grading to focus on inspiring minds.

The Reality We're Changing

This shouldn't be the reality for any child. We're changing that.
